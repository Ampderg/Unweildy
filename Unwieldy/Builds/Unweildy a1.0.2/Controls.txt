KEYBOARD/CONTROLLER
keyboard controls red player, controller controls blue player

A+D/LEFT JOYSTICK to move left and right
SPACE/A to jump, hold to jump higher

Hammer:
LEFT CLICK/X to do a light attack, 
   in the air it attacks in the direction your mouse or joystick is pointing
SHIFT/Y to do a launch attach
HOLD RIGHT CLICK/B to do a charge up attack, RELEASE to swing a devestating blow
   that launches you backwards
CONTROL/BUMPER to drop your weapon

Unarmed:
LEFT CLICK/X to do a weak punch
SHIFT/Y to do a temporarily invincible roll
HOLD RIGHT CLICK/B to pick up a dropped weapon
